Our Programmes

At Samtse College of Education (SCE), we offer a variety of programmes designed to prepare professional teachers and educators for Bhutan’s education system.

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)

The B.Ed programme prepares future teachers with strong pedagogical knowledge and teaching methodologies for primary and secondary schools in Bhutan.

Master of Education (M.Ed)

The M.Ed programme equips educators with advanced skills in teaching, research, and leadership to strengthen Bhutan’s education sector.

Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PgDE)

The PgDE is offered to graduates from various disciplines who aspire to join the teaching profession and contribute to secondary education in Bhutan.

Postgraduate Diploma in Education (Dzongkha)

This programme trains Dzongkha graduates to become teachers and helps preserve Bhutan’s national language and culture.
